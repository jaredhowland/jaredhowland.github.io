# Projects
A more complete list of my projects can be found on my [Vita][1].

## [Journal Overlap Database][2]

I created a database that allowed us to determine the number of places we had access to all our journals. This allowed us to see overlap and cancel subscriptions without losing access to any content.

## [Facebook Application][3]
I created a Facebook application that searches ScholarSearch and allows for manipulation of the results by saving to a folder.
